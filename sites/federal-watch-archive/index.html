Research Mission

The Federal Watch Archive provides rigorous, fact-based analysis of declassified government documents and accountability research. As a former intelligence analyst, I apply professional investigative standards to examine how federal agencies operate and serve the public interest.

My approach emphasizes:
  • Primary source documentation from official channels
  • Cross-verification through multiple independent sources
  • Professional analytical methods from intelligence community
  • Clear distinction between verified facts and analysis
  • Academic-quality citations and source attribution
"Democracy requires transparency. Citizens deserve access to the facts about their government."
- Dr. Patricia Caldwell
