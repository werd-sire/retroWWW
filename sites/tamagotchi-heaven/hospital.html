🩺 SYMPTOM RECOGNITION GUIDE

Learn to identify the warning signs of Tamagotchi illness:
🚨 CRITICAL SYMPTOMS (Immediate Treatment Required):
💀 Skull Icon: Most obvious sign of illness
😰 Weak/Sad Expression: Pet looks lethargic
🚫 Refuses Food: Won't eat when hungry
🔇 Weak Beeping: Sounds different from normal
🏃‍♂️ Won't Play: Refuses games when unhappy
⚠️ WARNING SYMPTOMS (Monitor Closely):
😕 Frequent Unhappiness: Happiness drops quickly
🍽️ Irregular Eating: Sometimes refuses food
😴 Excessive Sleeping: Sleeps more than usual
📉 Slow Growth: Not evolving on schedule
⚖️ Weight Fluctuation: Rapid weight changes
📊 SEVERITY ASSESSMENT:
MILD: 1-2 symptoms, responds to basic care
MODERATE: 3-4 symptoms, needs medicine
SEVERE: 5+ symptoms, requires intensive treatment
CRITICAL: Skull icon + multiple symptoms
💊 DR. KEVIN'S TREATMENT PROTOCOLS

🚑 EMERGENCY PROTOCOL (Skull Icon Present):
STEP 1: Remain calm - panic leads to mistakes!
STEP 2: Locate medicine icon (syringe, pill, or medicine bottle)
STEP 3: Administer first dose of medicine
STEP 4: Wait 10-15 minutes (time is critical!)
STEP 5: Give second dose if skull still present
STEP 6: Monitor closely for next 30 minutes
STEP 7: Third dose if no improvement

⚠️ If skull persists after 3 doses, contact Dr. Kevin IMMEDIATELY!
🏥 STANDARD CARE PROTOCOL (No Skull):
ASSESSMENT: Check hunger, happiness, weight, and discipline
NUTRITION: Feed if hunger below 3 hearts
EXERCISE: Play games if happiness below 3 hearts
HYGIENE: Clean up any waste immediately
MONITORING: Check every 30 minutes for 2 hours
FOLLOW-UP: Return to normal care schedule if stable
🔄 RECOVERY MONITORING:
HOUR 1: Check every 15 minutes
HOUR 2-4: Check every 30 minutes
HOUR 5-24: Check every hour
DAY 2+: Return to normal schedule

RECOVERY SIGNS: Normal appetite, active playing, stable weight
RELAPSE SIGNS: Return of any original symptoms
