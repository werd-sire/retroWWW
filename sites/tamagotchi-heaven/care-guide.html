🥚 TAMAGOTCHI BASICS

What is a Tamagotchi?
A Tamagotchi is a digital pet that lives inside a small egg-shaped device. It was invented by Bandai in Japan and means "lovable egg." Your job is to take care of it like a real pet!

The Life Cycle:
🥚 Egg Stage: Lasts about 1 hour (just wait!)
🐣 Baby Stage: 1 hour, very easy to care for
🐤 Child Stage: 2-3 days, starts getting personality
🐦 Teen Stage: 2-4 days, needs more attention
👑 Adult Stage: 4-6 days, final evolution based on your care!
💀 Death: Usually around day 10-15 if you're lucky

The Three Main Stats:
❤️ Hunger: How full your pet is (4 hearts = full)
😊 Happiness: How happy your pet is (4 hearts = very happy)
💪 Discipline: How well-behaved your pet is (affects evolution)
💡 KEVIN'S PRO TIP: The discipline stat is invisible but SUPER important for getting good evolutions! You need about 75% discipline for the best adults!
🍎 FEEDING YOUR TAMAGOTCHI

MEAL TYPE WHEN TO USE EFFECT KEVIN'S NOTES
🍎 MEAL When hunger hearts are empty Fills 1 hunger heart Use bread first, then meal if still hungry
🍞 SNACK When slightly hungry Fills 1 hunger heart, makes pet happier Don't overfeed - causes overweight!

🕐 FEEDING SCHEDULE (Kevin's Method):
7:00 AM: Wake up, check pet, feed if hungry
12:00 PM: Lunch break feeding (usually needs it)
3:30 PM: After school check (CRITICAL TIME!)
6:00 PM: Dinner time feeding
9:00 PM: Before bed final check

⚖️ WEIGHT MANAGEMENT:
Normal weight is around 30-35 oz. If your pet gets over 40 oz, stop giving snacks and play more games to lose weight. Fat pets evolve into less cute characters!
⚠️ FEEDING MISTAKES TO AVOID:
• Don't feed when all hunger hearts are full (waste of food!)
• Don't give only snacks (unbalanced diet!)
• Don't forget to feed for more than 6 hours (THEY DIE!)
• Don't overfeed just because they're beeping (might need attention, not food!)
🎮 PLAYING GAMES

Games serve multiple purposes: they increase happiness, help your pet lose weight, and are super fun! There are usually 2 games available.

🎯 GUESSING GAME (Left/Right):
Your pet hides behind either the left or right side. Guess correctly to win! This is pure luck, so don't feel bad if you lose.

🔢 NUMBER GAME:
Usually involves guessing numbers or timing. Some versions have different mini-games. Practice makes perfect!

🏆 GAME STRATEGY:
• Play when happiness hearts are low
• Play to help overweight pets lose pounds
• Win to increase happiness faster
• Lose to decrease weight faster (if needed)
• Don't play if pet is hungry (feed first!)
💡 SECRET GAME TIPS:
• Some pets have patterns to their hiding
• The timing game gets faster as your pet ages
• Playing games right before evolution time can influence the outcome
• Sick pets can't play games (give medicine first!)
👨‍🏫 DISCIPLINE & TRAINING

This is the HARDEST part of Tamagotchi care! Discipline affects what your pet evolves into, but it's invisible so you can't see your progress.

😤 WHEN TO DISCIPLINE:
• When your pet beeps but doesn't need food, games, or medicine
• When your pet refuses to eat when hungry
• When your pet refuses to play when unhappy
• When your pet has a "naughty" expression

😊 WHEN TO PRAISE:
• When your pet does something good
• When your pet is being cute
• When your pet successfully plays a game
• Random praise throughout the day

⚖️ DISCIPLINE BALANCE:
Too little discipline = Bad evolution (usually Kusatchi)
Perfect discipline (about 75%) = Good evolution (Mimitchi, Ginjirotchi)
Too much discipline = Also bad evolution (stressed pets!)
⚠️ DISCIPLINE MISTAKES:
• Don't discipline when your pet legitimately needs something
• Don't ignore calling for attention (this builds bad habits)
• Don't over-discipline (makes pet stressed)
• Don't forget that praise is just as important as discipline!
💊 HEALTH & MEDICINE

💀 SICKNESS SYMPTOMS:
• Skull icon appears next to your pet
• Pet looks sad or lethargic
• Pet refuses to eat or play
• Pet makes different beeping sounds

💊 GIVING MEDICINE:
1. Select the medicine icon (usually a needle or pill)
2. Give medicine immediately when skull appears
3. Sometimes takes 2-3 doses to cure
4. Don't give medicine to healthy pets (makes them sick!)

🛡️ PREVENTING SICKNESS:
• Keep hunger and happiness high
• Don't let pet get overweight
• Clean up poop immediately (hygiene!)
• Don't leave pet unfed for long periods
• Maintain good discipline
💡 HEALTH SECRETS:
• Sick pets age slower (silver lining!)
• Some evolutions are only possible if your pet gets sick and recovers
• Medicine works faster if given early
• Very sick pets might die even with medicine!
🎓 ADVANCED TECHNIQUES

🔄 PAUSE FUNCTION:
Some people pause their Tamagotchi to stop aging, but I think this is cheating! Real pets don't pause. However, pausing during school or sleep is acceptable for emergencies.

⏰ TIME MANIPULATION:
You can change the clock to adjust your pet's schedule, but this can mess up evolution timing. I don't recommend it unless you're desperate.

🧬 EVOLUTION MANIPULATION:
• Good care (high hunger/happiness, 75% discipline) = Mimitchi/Ginjirotchi
• Average care = Kuchipatchi/Nyorotchi
• Poor care = Kusatchi/Tarakotchi
• Sick care = Special secret evolutions sometimes!

🏆 GENERATION BREEDING:
When your adult pet reaches about 6-7 years old, it might have a baby! The baby inherits some traits from the parent, so good care continues through generations.
⚠️ ADVANCED WARNINGS:
• Don't reset unless your pet dies (you lose everything!)
• Don't use time cheats excessively (breaks the experience)
• Don't neglect your pet just to get a "bad" character
• Some rare evolutions require very specific care patterns!
