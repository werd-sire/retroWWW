⚠️ CRITICAL TAMAGOTCHI SURVIVAL INFO:

⏰ FEEDING SCHEDULE: Every 4 hours or they get sick!
🎮 GAME STRATEGY: Win to increase happiness, lose weight
💊 MEDICINE: Give immediately when sick (skull icon)
🛡️ DISCIPLINE: Only when they're being naughty (ignore beeping)
