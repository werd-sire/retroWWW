💡 KEVIN'S SECRET EVOLUTION TIPS! 💡
🎯 PRO STRATEGIES:

🕐 TIMING MATTERS: Evolution happens during sleep cycles (around 10 PM). Never wake your pet during evolution night!

🎮 GAME INFLUENCE: Winning lots of games right before evolution can sometimes tip the scales toward better characters!

💊 SICKNESS STRATEGY: Some advanced players intentionally make their pet sick once and cure it to unlock certain evolution paths.

🔄 GENERATION EFFECTS: Second generation+ pets can have slightly different evolution requirements based on their parent's final form.

⚖️ DISCIPLINE TIMING: Discipline works best during the teen stage. Baby and child discipline doesn't count as much!

🍎 FEEDING PATTERNS: Overfeeding can block good evolutions even with perfect happiness and discipline!

🔮 KEVIN'S THEORY: I think the exact time you discipline matters. I get better results disciplining at specific times of day!
