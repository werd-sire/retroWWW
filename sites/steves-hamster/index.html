cool hamster facts!!!

  • hamsters are rodents but they are cuter than rats
  • they originally come from syria which is in the middle east
  • baby hamsters are called pups and they are really tiny
  • hamsters have really good hearing but bad eyesight
  • they can run up to 5 miles per night on their wheel!!!

how to take care of a hamster

if you want to get a hamster like mr whiskers here is what you need to know:

1. you need a big cage with lots of room to run around
2. you need a exercise wheel so they can run at night
3. you need wood shavings for bedding but NOT cedar because it makes them sick
4. you need a water bottle and a food dish
5. you need hamster food from the pet store
6. you can give them treats like sunflower seeds and carrots
7. you have to clean their cage every week or it will smell really bad
8. you have to be gentle when you pick them up or they might bite you
9. you should never give them chocolate or onions because it will kill them
10. you have to love them and take good care of them like i do with mr whiskers!!!
