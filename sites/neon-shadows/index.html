Welcome to the Shadow Zone!

Greetings, rock warriors! You've found your way to the official home of The Neon Shadows, the tri-state area's hottest alternative rock sensation!

We're four guys from nowhere making music for everywhere. Our sound blends the raw power of grunge with the energy of punk and just a touch of that electronic edge that's gonna define the new millennium.
