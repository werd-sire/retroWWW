Recording Studio Chronicles

Where We Record:
• Tony's Basement Studio (Mike's cousin) - 8-track setup
• Cosmic Studios, Newark NJ - Professional 16-track

Our Equipment:
• Shure SM57 microphones
• Tascam 8-track recorder
• Various guitar amps and pedals
• Pearl drum kit

Recording Process:
We usually record live as a band, then add vocal overdubs and guitar solos. Keeps the energy and spontaneity of our live shows!

Future Plans:
Saving up for professional studio time at Water Music in Hoboken. Goal: Record full album in spring 2000!
