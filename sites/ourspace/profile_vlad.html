About Vladimir

Привет! (Hello!) I'm Vladimir, the founder and creator of OurSpace. I started this project in my apartment in Moscow with a simple vision: to connect people across the world through the power of the internet.

I studied Computer Science at Moscow State University, where I became fascinated with the potential of social networking. After working for several Russian tech companies, I decided to create something new - a platform where people could truly express themselves and build meaningful connections.

OurSpace launched in January 2003, and we've grown to nearly a million users worldwide! What started as a weekend project has become my life's work. Our team is now 47 people strong, working from our offices in Moscow and St. Petersburg.

When I'm not coding or managing the company, I enjoy playing chess in Gorky Park, exploring Moscow's incredible food scene, and traveling to meet our international users. I'm also learning Mandarin - China is our fastest-growing market!

My Philosophy: The internet should bring people together, not divide them. OurSpace is built on principles of openness, creativity, and authentic human connection.

Feel free to send me a message - I read every single one!
- Владимир (Vladimir)

Blog Entry - December 20, 2003

Subject: "OurSpace Hits 800,000 Users!"

 Incredible news, everyone! We just hit 800,000 registered users on OurSpace. When I started coding this in my small apartment two years ago, I never imagined we'd grow this fast.

This week we're launching several new features:
• Enhanced photo albums (up to 100 photos per user!)
• Improved messaging system with file attachments
• Mobile access for WAP-enabled phones
• Group forums with better moderation tools

Special thanks to our development team: Alexei, Natasha, Igor, and Katya. You're making the impossible possible every day.

Also exciting - we're opening our San Francisco office next month! This will help us better serve our growing American user base.
