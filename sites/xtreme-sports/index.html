📍 LOCAL SKATE SPOTS 📍
THE SICKEST SPOTS IN TOWN!
  • Downtown Office Building: 15 stair rail, security doesn't care on weekends!
  • High School Steps: Perfect for practicing ollies, watch out for Principal Johnson!
  • Mall Parking Lot: Smooth concrete, cool security guard named Dave
  • City Park Halfpipe: Kinda sketchy but rideable, needs new coping
  • Warehouse District: Underground scene, sick street course, BYOB
  • School Parking Garage: Good for rainy days, echo makes tricks sound SICK!

• Always wear a helmet (I know, I know, but don't be stupid)
• Check for security before you start your session
• Respect the spots - don't leave trash or wax everywhere
• Bring a first aid kit (trust me on this one)
• Don't skate alone in sketchy areas
