@PokeTrainer99 - Not every anime needs giant robots! Cowboy Bebop is more sophisticated. It's aimed at older viewers who appreciate:
- Complex, flawed characters - Philosophical themes (existentialism, loneliness, the past haunting you) - Cinematic storytelling - Jazz fusion soundtracks - Mature themes without being edgy
No disrespect to Pokemon (I love Pokemon!), but Cowboy Bebop is operating on a different level. It's like comparing a Saturday morning cartoon to a Scorsese film.
Both are good! But one is art.
