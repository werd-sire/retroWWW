Check out these sites:

AnimeNation.com - Great selection, official releases
RightStuf.com - HUGE catalog, frequent sales
AnimeCastle.com - Good prices, fast shipping

All three are legit. They sell official Pioneer/Bandai/ADV releases.

Fair warning: Anime VHS can be EXPENSIVE. Like $20-30 per tape with only 2-3 episodes. But that's how we support the industry!

Also: Check out Suncoast Video at the mall. They usually have a decent anime section.
