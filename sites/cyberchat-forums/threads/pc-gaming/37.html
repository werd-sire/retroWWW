Okay, I'm calling it:

Half-Life is the best first-person shooter ever created.

Here's why:
- Story-driven gameplay (no cut scenes interrupting action)
- Intelligent AI (Marines actually flank you!)
- Immersive atmosphere (Black Mesa feels REAL)
- Seamless level transitions (no loading between areas)
- Physics-based puzzles
- The crowbar (iconic)
- Headcrabs (terrifying and fun)

Quake and Doom are great, but they're just "run and gun". Half-Life is an EXPERIENCE.

Prove me wrong. I'll wait.
