December 27, 1999 - 11:15 AM EST
United Airlines and American Airlines will ground all flights from 11:30 PM on December 31st until 6:00 AM on January 1st as a "precautionary measure" while navigation systems are tested. Flight booking systems have experienced intermittent failures during stress tests. Check Flight Status

December 26, 1999 - 4:22 PM EST
Regional power companies across the United States report varying levels of Y2K compliance. While 78% of utilities claim full readiness, several smaller companies admit to ongoing issues with SCADA systems and automated controls. Rolling blackouts possible in some areas. Check Your Area
