🎵 Concert Reviews & Experiences

🎤 AMAZING SHOW - November 1999
Venue: Local Arena
My Seat: Row 15 (so close I could see their facial expressions!)
Outfit: Pink glittery top with my homemade "I LOVE YOU" sign

The harmonies were PERFECT and the choreography was so synchronized! During the slow song, I swear the cute one looked right at me! My voice is completely gone from screaming but it was SO worth it! 🎶
Rating: ♥♥♥♥♥ (BEST NIGHT OF MY LIFE!)
🎪 Summer Concert Series - August 1999
Venue: Outdoor Amphitheater
Weather: Hot but I didn't care!
Special Moment: Got a guitar pick thrown into the crowd!

Even though it was 95 degrees, nothing could ruin this night! The acoustic version of their ballad made me cry happy tears. I'm definitely going to frame that guitar pick! 🎸
Rating: ♥♥♥♥☆ (Only 4 hearts because I got sunburned!)
