What is a Web Ring?

A web ring is a circular collection of related websites! Each site displays navigation links that allow visitors to travel to the next, previous, or a random site in the ring. It's a great way to discover new sites and connect with others who share your interests!

Benefits of Joining:
• Increase traffic to your site!
• Connect with like-minded webmasters!
• Be part of a community!
• Get discovered by new visitors!
