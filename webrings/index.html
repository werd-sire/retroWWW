What Are Web Rings?

Web rings are organized groups of websites that share similar themes or interests. Each member site displays navigation links that allow visitors to easily move between sites in the ring.

Benefits of Joining:
• Increased traffic to your site
• Connect with like-minded webmasters
• Be part of a community
• Share audiences and cross-promote
• Discover new sites in your interest area

How Navigation Works:
• Next: Go to the next site in the ring
• Previous: Go to the previous site in the ring
• Random: Jump to a random site in the ring
• List: View all sites in the ring
