About This Ring

The Alternative Music Web Ring connects bands, musicians, and music fans across the World Wide Web. Whether you're into grunge, punk, indie, metal, or experimental sounds, this ring is your gateway to discovering new music and connecting with fellow music lovers.

Founded in November 1999, we're dedicated to supporting independent and alternative musicians who are using the web to share their art with the world.
