Submission Guidelines

Before submitting your website, please ensure it meets our quality standards:

• Site must be complete and functional
• Content must be appropriate for all audiences
• No broken links or under construction pages
• Site must load properly in Internet Explorer 4.0+ and Netscape 4.0+
• No excessive pop-up windows or intrusive advertising
• Original content preferred over link collections

Review Process: All submissions are manually reviewed by our editorial staff within 5-7 business days. Free submissions may take longer during busy periods.
